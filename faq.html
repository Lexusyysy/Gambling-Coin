1. What is Moon Friends?


Moon Friends is a community-oriented meta franchise - Moons that have fallen from the space to earth and trying to get back to where they belong to by the help of the community. The project's main goal is to build a web-3 franchise with a strong and long-lasting legacy by enhancing storytelling experiences and integrating web-3 interactive channels.
